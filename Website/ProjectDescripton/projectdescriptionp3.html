Roles


Each team member of our group has specific roles that tailor towards their strengths and desires. Due to our group consisting of only three people, we made the decision to diversify our roles in multiple different areas as we may need to work on some parts together, or by ourselves. If we were to specify specifically what each other’s roles are, then our team member Dawn Aquino would be the lead user interface designer as she has expressed her strong skills in that key area. That is not to say that she would be doing all the designing, we as a team would make decisions together if she believes she needs feedback or input by the team. Alfred Hutomo and I, Carlin, would be the lead software developers in that we would be writing the application in java. Scope and limits


Given the large range of cinemas that exist in international countries, this product is most likely going to be exclusive to Australian users only, as there would to many movie theatres to consider and to hold information for; if the product were made for global consumption. To refine it further, Popper will only hold and relay information regarding the main three movie theatres in Australia, which are Hoyts, Event and Village cinemas. As there will be enough data to proof its use and function to users.


One of the features of the product is, that it uses offers directions to the cinema of interest however it will only show directions of the place of interest not anything else. It will not function to serve directions to everything as google maps does. As well as Popper’s ability to relay information would also be limited, it would only have data regarding movie theatres and movies so hence anything out the realm of the movies industry would be a limit for the product as no data is stored or accessed for such information.
